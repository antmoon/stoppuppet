About "tyranny's Puppet"

This project is a bold statement against political figures who choose to align with Putin's regime, ignoring the suffering of innocent lives. We use satire, humor, and powerful visuals to expose their complicity.

The goal is simple: raise awareness, unite people, and demand justice for Ukraine and its people.

In the face of tyranny and aggression, we must ask ourselves: who stands with the oppressed, and who stands with the oppressor? The "Putin's Puppet" meme project is more than just a critique; it’s a bold statement of solidarity with the people of Ukraine, whose lives, homes, and futures have been torn apart by Putin's unprovoked invasion.

When influential figures like Donald Trump choose to align with a brutal dictator over the freedom and sovereignty of a nation, it's not just politics — it's a betrayal of human decency. "Putin's Puppet" pulls back the curtain, exposing the truth that some are willing to dance to Putin's strings, while millions suffer in silence.

This project is about more than memes — it’s about giving a voice to those whose cries for help echo in the darkness. It’s about standing up against the tide of disinformation and manipulation, and reminding the world that peace, justice, and freedom must always come before political games.

Through sharp, unapologetic humor and piercing visuals, "Putin's Puppet" seeks to shine a light on those who have chosen to ignore the suffering of innocent people, and to call for change. Because in this fight for the future, silence is complicity.
